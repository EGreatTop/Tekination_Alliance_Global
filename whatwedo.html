Datacenter Management

We provide datacenter management and support services, proactive monitoring while continually leveraging data-driven technologies. Our Data center management includes proactive monitoring and visualizing the details and activities across all systems and locations. Analysis can then be carried out on how to utilize the data center more efficiently to save energy and space or increase the utilization of existing equipment. Data Center Infrastructure Management (DCIM) solutions are used to monitor, measure, and manage data centers, covering both IT equipment and supporting infrastructure such as power and cooling systems. The following are some of the monitored equipment: Racks, PDUs, Switches, Cabling, Servers, Storage, Robots, Cameras, Door Locks, Sensors (temperature, humidity, water, smoke, airflow, air pressure, contact closure, etc.).




Site Technology Management

Our facilities management services ensures facilities/sites support functions as planned. We takes the principles of general facilities management and marries them to an array of important IT demands. The objectives of IT facilities management are clear:

  • • Optimize synergy between digital assets and workplace facilities
  • • Cooperate with IT support to install and service IT assets
  • • Manage the physical environment of vital IT infrastructure

    • There are a few areas where building management systems (BMS) can play an important role in the data Center:
    1. Physical Security.
    2. Lighting Control.
    3. Efficient Cooling.
    4. Power Distribution.
    5. Remote Monitoring Capability.
    6. Maintenance Scheduling.
    7. Enable Planning & Upgrades.
    8. Improve Efficiency - Data collected by the BMS can be critical to determining which measures will increase efficiency and (potentially) by how much.
