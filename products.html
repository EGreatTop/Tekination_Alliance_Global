About Rubrik

Rubrik simplifies data protection with a next- generation architecture. It offers the industry’s first Converged Cloud Data Management appliance that delivers backup, recovery, replication, and data archival. Rubrik eliminates the complexity of legacy enterprise backup and Disaster Recovery. It collapses backup software, catalogue management, replication, and de-duplicated storage into a single appliance that scales linearly. It’s built on a distributed architecture, which means de-duplication and other data services scale in-line with the cluster to maximize efficiency and savings. With Rubrik, users can deliver near-zero recovery times without rehydration to primary systems.












About SevOne

SevOne provides the only network monitoring platform, engineered for Speed at Scale. The patented SevOne Cluster architecture leverages distributed computing to scale infinitely and collect millions of objects to provide real-time reporting down to the second and help organizations prevent outages.












About Uplogix

Uplogix was founded on the observation that the problem with network management is that it relied on the network itself to operate. To really manage network infrastructure, you need to operate from an out-of-band perspective and more than just as a remote secondary connection to devices. For Uplogix, evolving out-of-band means monitoring devices and taking actions directly over the console port, like an onsite technician plugging in a laptop. Uplogix go far beyond traditional “dumb” console servers that sit plugged in and waiting to be useful should there be a crisis.












About Attivo Networks

The most advanced approach for detecting threats within the network by setting traps to misdirect and lure attackers into revealing themselves. Attackers can and will get inside your network, therefore Attivo offers powerful visibility through real-time detection of in-network threats and accelerate incident handling, reducing time and budget spent chasing false alarms and simplifying incident response. Scales easily & deploy quickly to large user networks, data centres, cloud and specialty environments.
